### Citation Style for Emails
- Informal: *"One mom told us, '[quote]'"*
- Integrated: *"We hear this a lot from parents — '[quote]'"*
- Lena voice: *"I remember feeling this way too — '[paraphrased quote]'"*

### Critical Rules
- NEVER fabricate quotes. Every quote in this bank is verbatim from source.
- Preserve parent language — dialect, hesitations, emphasis.
- If altering age/gender for anonymity, stay within Level 1 range (3-5 years).
- Category 5 quote "He gets it, but he doesn't get it" is the single most powerful Level 1 recognition anchor — use it sparingly for maximum impact.
- The "He's not 'autistic enough' for autism methods" quote (Supplemental, #79) is the Level 1 identity crisis phrase — use in high-empathy moments only.
