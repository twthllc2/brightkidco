**Implication:** A parent facing the Triple Cluster (SPD+GI+Sleep) needs fundamentally different messaging than one with SPD+ADHD alone.

## 9. CORE DESIRE & TRANSFORMATION SUMMARY BY AVATAR

### Autistic/Neurodivergent Parent

- **Core Desire:** Safety, protection, dignity for child; social acceptance; competence as a mother; relief from constant vigilance
- **Transformation:** Chaos → Finding what works for THEIR child → Relief and confidence
- **Buy Trigger:** "This works for MY kind of kid"
- **Dominant Level:** L1–L3 (splits across all levels based on child's severity)
- **Dominant Phase:** Phase 2–4 depending on journey length

### Deadline Pressure Parent (Pre-K/Preschool)

- **Core Desire:** Security, stability, access to education, control over timeline, relief from uncertainty
- **Transformation:** Panic → Quick solution → School enrollment secured
- **Buy Trigger:** "This will get my child trained BEFORE school starts"
- **Dominant Level:** L1 (dominant), L2 (possible)
- **Dominant Phase:** Phase 2 (high conversion window)

### Social Pressure Parent (Comparison-Driven)

- **Core Desire:** Social validation, belonging, competence as mother, avoiding shame, not being "behind"
- **Transformation:** Shame → Validation → Pride
- **Buy Trigger:** "I don't want to be the mom whose kid is still in diapers"
- **Dominant Level:** L1 (dominant), L2 (common), L3 (possible)
- **Dominant Phase:** Phase 2–3

### Overwhelmed At-Home Mom (Crisis Mode)

- **Core Desire:** Sanity, order, relief from chaos, emotional regulation, not feeling like a failure in her own home
- **Transformation:** Survival mode → Less cleanup → Sanity restored
- **Buy Trigger:** "This will reduce the mess and chaos TODAY"
- **Dominant Level:** L2 (dominant), L3 (common)
- **Dominant Phase:** Phase 3–4
