### LEVEL 3 (High Support Needs, 6-10+ years)

**Partner conflict transforms into SOLO DECISION-MAKING.** Listed as item #28.

**Key dynamics:**
- ~20-25% single moms at Level 3

- No "make this together" framing
- Solo decision validation is critical

- "No need to convince anyone — just try"

## 6. EMOTIONAL LANDMINES IN EMAIL COPY

### Things That Trigger Partner Conflict Pain:
1. "Discuss with your partner first" — assumes partner is involved and supportive
2. "Make this together as a family" — ignores solo decision reality
3. "Get both parents on board" — impossible for many moms
4. Implying co-parent is undermining — even if true, don't say it
5. Aggressive language against co-parent — mom may still love partner

### Things That Defuse Partner Conflict Pain:
1. "You don't need anyone's permission to try this"
2. "60-day guarantee means you can try without commitment"
3. "Even if your co-parent is more cautious..." — acknowledges reality without blame
4. "You know your kid better than anyone"
5. "No need to convince anyone — just try"
6. "Daytime trials at home can start small" — low-stakes, no partner buy-in needed
