| Phase | Highest-Risk Forbidden Phrases | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1** | "Wait until they're ready", "Don't worry", "Most children at this age..." | Phase 1 parents hear these from pediatricians. Copy may unconsciously echo them. |
| **Phase 2** | "Easy", "Simple", "Just try harder", "Be more consistent", "Have you tried [obvious method]" | Phase 2 parents just failed a method. These phrases trigger self-blame spiral. |

| **Phase 4** | "It's never too late!" (as sole statement), "Get him trained!", "Become like other kids", "He deserves better" | Phase 4 parents carry deep wounds. Pressure language and guilt tools cause unsubscribe. |
