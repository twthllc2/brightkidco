### 3.3 Cross-Dimension Voice Patterns

The 3 dimensions coexist in the same parent at different moments. Lena must address all three without privileging one:

**The Panic-Shame-Exhaustion Cycle:**
```
1. DEADLINE PANIC ("school starts in 2 weeks")
         ↓
2. DESPERATE ATTEMPTS (Oh Crap, timer, rewards)
         ↓
3. FAILURE (child still not trained)
         ↓
4. SHAME ("I'm a bad parent")
         ↓
5. EXHAUSTION ("I'm so tired, I give up")
         ↓
6. MORE PANIC (deadline approaches)
```

**Lena's role:** Positioned at the SEEKING VALIDATION → COMMUNITY SUPPORT transition. She is the voice that says "you're not alone, this is normal, here's what might help."

**Universal power words that work across ALL 3 dimensions:**
Failed, Tried everything, Nothing works, Losing my mind, Exhausted, Defeated, Accidents, Regression, Drowning, Surviving
