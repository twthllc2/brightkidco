## CATEGORY D: AGAINST HERSELF (D1-D4)

### D1 — "I've tried everything. Why would this be different?"

**INTENSITY:** VERY HIGH

**EXACT THOUGHT PATTERN:**
"My husband and I had tried various methods over the past 3 years. We used picture symbols, social stories, behavioral contingency plans. None of these methods worked."

**COUNTER-STRATEGY LOGIC:**
1. Name each method explicitly (sticker chart, social story, ABA, naked method, scheduled sits, timers, 4 different potty seats)
2. Explain WHY each one failed (sensory gap argument)
3. Reframe: "You haven't failed. You solved the wrong problem."

**LANGUAGE THAT WORKS (VERBATIM):**
"Sticker charts. Social stories. Timed sits. ABA protocols. 4 different potty seats. We've heard about all of them. You already know they don't work for your kid. Here's why none of them COULD work."

**AVATAR LEVEL APPLICABILITY:**
- Level 1: MEDIUM-HIGH (1-3 failed attempts, less intense)
- Level 2: VERY HIGH (3-5+ attempts, BCBA cycling, deeply resonant)
- Level 3: HIGH (5+ years of attempts including residential programs)
- General Fallback: VERY HIGH (universal frustration)

**ADDITIONAL VERBATIM LANGUAGE FROM LEVEL DOCS:**

Level 1 (DOC2):
- "Oh Crap stressed us both out."
- "He hated the sticker chart by day 3."
- "We did everything the book said and it just didn't click."
- "His OT recommended timed sits — they made him hate the bathroom."
- "We tried 3 different potty seats. He likes them all but won't go in any."
- "We did naked weekends. He just held it for 8 hours then pooped on the rug."

Level 2 (DOC3):
- "We've been doing scheduled sits for 2 years. He never goes on his own."
- "BCBA protocol got us to 'sit on the toilet without melting down.' That's it. No actual training."
- "OT sensory work helped with other things, but not the bathroom."
- "Social stories made her more confused, not less."
- "We did a 'no pull-up' day. He held it for 11 hours then peed everywhere."
- "I tried bribery, rewards, sticker charts, social stories, ABA protocols, scheduled sits, weighted blankets, special seats. Nothing changes the fundamental issue."
- "Naked therapy at the autism center — meltdowns daily, no progress."
- "We hired a private potty consultant for $2000. Same result."

Level 3 (DOC4):
- "We did 40 hours of ABA a week for 2 years. He's still in pull-ups."
- "We even did a residential program for 3 weeks. He's back in pull-ups within a month of home."
- "His BCBA has worked with him for 6 years. No initiation."
- "We spent $2500 on a private potty consultant. Same result."
- "I've tried everything. I mean it this time, not as a phrase."

**CONVERSION LEVER PAIRING:** Lever 1 ("It's not the method") — VERY HIGH for all levels

**USE IN FLOWS:** Welcome E2-E4, FAQ E1, Cart Abandonment E2
