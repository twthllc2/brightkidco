## 6. OFFER × AVATAR LEVEL MAPPING

### Level 1 (Verbal, 3-5, Pre-K-driven)
- **Default offer:** 3+3 Bundle
- **Key lever:** Pre-K deadline urgency ("Pre-K won't take him in pull-ups")
- **60-day guarantee:** VERY HIGH importance — hope-risk absorber
- **Math reframe:** OK (carefully, not preachy)
- **No Medicaid pull-up routine** (less price sensitivity)
- **Conversion phases:** Phase 2 highest (~15-25%), Phase 3 medium-high (~10-18%)
- **Cart recovery E1:** 15-20% recovery rate
- **Best timing:** Spring/Summer (Pre-K enrollment deadline)
- **Source:** DOC2 L200, L255, L299, L336, L858, L899, L908

### Level 2 (Mixed, 5-7, BCBA context — LARGEST segment)
- **Default offer:** 3+3 Bundle
- **Key lever:** BCBA-complementary positioning ("ABA works for behavior, BodySignal works for sensory")
- **60-day guarantee:** VERY HIGH — permission to try alongside BCBA
- **Math reframe:** Carefully (financial sensitivity present)
- **Bundle size hint:** 3+3 → committed, 5+5 → high-investment
- **Cart recovery:** Slightly lower than Level 1
  - E1 (1h): 55-65% Open, 10-15% Recovery
  - E2 (24h): 35-45% Open, 6-10% Recovery
  - E3 (72h): 25-35% Open, 4-7% Recovery
- **Source:** DOC3 L1318-1321, L1405-1406, L1414-1417

### Level 3 (Non-verbal, 6-10+, Dignity-First)
- **Default offer:** 1 Pair (skeptical audience) OR 3+3 (if trigger-driven)
- **Key levers:** Adult diaper size approaching, school transition, puberty, late-trainer stories
- **60-day guarantee:** VERY HIGH — but framed as "try alongside" not "replace"
- **Math reframe:** NEVER — Medicaid reality for ~25%
- **Pull-up framing:** "Add alongside" NOT "abandon pull-ups" — Medicaid-funded, existential
- **Decision period:** 14-30 days (significantly longer than other levels)
- **Tone:** NO urgency, never. Science + dignity in balance.
- **Conversion triggers:** External events (adult diaper size, school, puberty, GI crisis)
- **Source:** DOC4 L345-346, L700-760, DOC1 L789-791, DOC6 L582-584

### General Fallback (Cross-level / Unknown)
- **Default offer:** 3+3 Bundle
- **Strategy:** Three-testimonial pattern (one per level) — everyone finds themselves
- **No level labels** — symptom descriptions instead
- **Math reframe:** OK (same as Level 1)
- **Source:** DOC5 L589-621, DOC6 L572

## 19. OBJECTION COUNTERS (Offer-Related)

### C3 — "Is this worth $30-50 when I've already wasted so much?"
- **Intensity:** MEDIUM
- **Counter:** Math reframe (carefully, not preachy) + 60-day refund eliminates risk + show what happens if you DON'T invest
- **Language:** "Pull-ups cost roughly $876 a year. And they're actively preventing learning. The 3+3 bundle is $79.99 with a 60-day guarantee. The math isn't the point — but if you want to do it, the math works."
- **Source:** DOC1 L696-708

### D3 — "What if I get my hopes up again and it fails?"
- **Intensity:** HIGH
- **Counter:** Don't promise success. Promise different mechanism. Intellectual engagement reduces emotional risk. 60-day guarantee as hope-risk absorber.
- **Language:** "We're not asking you to hope. We're asking you to understand WHY the other approaches didn't address the root issue. Then decide if this mechanism makes sense for your child."
- **Source:** DOC1 L642-657

### B1 — "I tried underwear and it didn't help either"
- **Intensity:** HIGH (MOST DANGEROUS — appears to disprove mechanism)
- **Counter:** Difference in signal duration & intensity. Plain underwear: brief, diffuse. Body-Signal Layer: sustained, localized 30-60s.
- **Source:** DOC1 L710-728
