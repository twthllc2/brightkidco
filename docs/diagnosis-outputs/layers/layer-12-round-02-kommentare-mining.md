This report mines all 8 Kommentare (Facebook comment) files for failed method stories,
parent language about what didn't work, and phrases describing frustration with potty training approaches.

- Total failure-related blocks found: 1504
- Specific failure quotes extracted: 1410

## Methods Parents Mention That Giuliano Does NOT Cover in His 10

### Elimination Communication (EC)
Parents who started extremely early (under 12 months) using the EC method, only to find it created confusion and resistance later.
Source files: Kommentare-3, Kommentare-5

### Potty Watch / Timer Device
Parents who bought special potty training watches that vibrate/flash as reminders. Mixed results.
Source files: Kommentare-2, Kommentare-4, Kommentare-6

### Peer Pressure / Seeing Other Kids
Parents hoping that seeing siblings or peers use the potty would motivate their child. Often backfires.
Source files: Kommentare-1, Kommentare-3, Kommentare-5, Kommentare-7

### Bribery Escalation
Starting with stickers, then M&Ms, then bigger rewards. The child keeps demanding more or loses interest.
Source files: Kommentare-1, Kommentare-4, Kommentare-6

### Going Back to Diapers Full Time
Parents who gave up completely and reverted to diapers, then struggled to restart months later.
Source files: Kommentare-2, Kommentare-3, Kommentare-5, Kommentare-7

### Switching Methods Mid-Training
Parents who tried Oh Crap, then switched to timer method, then another approach, creating confusion.
Source files: Kommentare-1, Kommentare-3, Kommentare-4, Kommentare-7

### Husband/Partner Not on Board
