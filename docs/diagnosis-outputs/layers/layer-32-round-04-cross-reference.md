## ALIGNMENT FINDINGS

### 1. Voice Formula — STRONG ALIGNMENT
The prototype emails follow the OBSERVATION → VALIDATION → MECHANISM → PERMISSION formula consistently:
- Email 1: Observation ("You haven't done anything wrong") + Validation
- Email 2: Mechanism (interoception explained)
- Email 3: Validation (failed methods reframed)
- Email 4: Mechanism (product layers explained)
- Email 5: Validation (parent story)
- Email 6: Permission (guarantee + no pressure)

This aligns with L9's core voice formula and the trust sequence pattern.

### 2. Content Rules — STRONG ALIGNMENT
Prototypes consistently follow these L9 voice rules:
- V1: Peer-to-peer tone (no corporate language)
- V5: No forbidden words ("easy", "simple", "quickly", etc.)
- V7: Validates failed methods before proposing mechanism
- V12: Permission-not-to-buy energy in CTAs
- V14: Realistic timelines (no speed claims)

### 3. Flow Architecture — ALIGNMENT
The 6-email welcome flow structure matches the planned architecture:
- 6 emails over 8 days (Flow 1)
- 4 emails for discount subscribers (Flow 2)
- Progressive trust building (education → mechanism → proof → invitation)

### 4. Component System — ALIGNMENT
The JSX component library provides a complete, reusable system:
- 3 tiers of product showcase (Soft/Medium/Full)
- Consistent section components (TitleBlock, Letter, ComparisonCard, etc.)
- Shared primitives (EmailShell, Header, Footer)

### 5. Copy Patterns — ALIGNMENT
Subject line and preheader patterns are well-designed:
- Curiosity hooks without clickbait
- Statement-style subjects (Flow 1)
- 3 A/B variants per email (Flow 2)
- Complementary preheaders that add emotional context

## CONFLICTS AND FLAGS

### CRITICAL CONFLICTS (Must Resolve Before Deployment)

#### C1: Color Palette Mismatch
**Prototype:** Custom palette (teal #2BAEB4, green #5DD07A, ink #1F2D2F)
**Brand Standard:** Brand palette (Brand-Green #039902, Mint #DBFFCD, Cream #FBF7EB)
**Impact:** Visual inconsistency with brand identity across all emails
**Resolution:** Adopt brand standard palette from L29/DOC6. Prototype colors must be remapped.

#### C2: Typography Mismatch
**Prototype:** Fraunces (display) + Inter (body) + Caveat (signatures)
**Brand Standard:** Questrial weight 400 ONLY for all text
**Impact:** Font inconsistency, potential rendering issues in email clients
**Resolution:** Replace all fonts with Questrial. Use system fallbacks (Arial/Helvetica) for email HTML.

#### C3: Max Width Mismatch
**Prototype:** 420px max width
**Brand Standard:** 600px max width
**Impact:** Emails will be narrower than standard, potentially wasting screen real estate
**Resolution:** Expand to 600px. This is an industry standard for email rendering.

#### C4: Sender Identity Violations
**Prototype Email 1:** "The BrightKidCo team" (violates V1)
**Prototype Flow 2:** "Lena, Founder" (violates V1 — Lena is Customer Support, NOT Founder)
**Brand Standard:** "Lena · Customer Support · Mom of two autistic sons"
**Impact:** Breaks peer-to-peer voice, undermines trust mechanic
**Resolution:** Replace all sign-offs with standard Lena identity per L9 §8.

#### C5: Trustpilot Reference
**Prototype Flow 2:** "4.9 · Trustpilot · 2,400+ reviews"
**Brand Standard:** NO Trustpilot links (DOC rule)
**Impact:** Links to non-existent or unmanaged page
**Resolution:** Remove Trustpilot references. Use "16,511 reviews · 4.9 ★" text-only format.

#### C6: Facebook Link in Footer
**Prototype:** Includes Facebook in social links
**Brand Standard:** Instagram + TikTok ONLY (NO Facebook)
**Impact:** Links to inactive or non-existent Facebook page
**Resolution:** Remove Facebook link. Keep Instagram + TikTok only.

#### C7: Discount Code Mismatch
**Prototype Flow 2:** BRIGHT10
**DOC6:** 10%OFF-BKCO
**Impact:** Code may not work in Klaviyo or may conflict with other promotions
**Resolution:** Clarify with client. Adopt one code and use consistently.

### MODERATE CONFLICTS (Should Resolve)

**Prototype:** €34 (Euro)
**Brand Standard:** $34.99 (USD)

#### C11: Send Timing Format

**Impact:** Ambiguous timing for Klaviyo implementation

#### C12: Footer Link URLs

**Brand Standard:** Specific URLs (brightkidco.com/collections/all-products, etc.)
**Impact:** Links may not work or may go to wrong pages
**Resolution:** Replace with exact URLs from L29 §1.5.

### Required Adaptations:

| **Sign-off Energy** | Encouraging | Empathetic | Gentle | Neutral |

### Component-Level Changes Needed:

2. **Testimonial Cards:** Must adjust length and format per level
3. **CTA Buttons:** Must adjust prominence and copy per level

5. **Section Length:** Must adjust whitespace and density per level
