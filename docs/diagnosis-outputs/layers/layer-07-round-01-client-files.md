## 9. CONVERSION BARRIERS PER SEGMENT (Detailed)

### Level 1 (Verbal, 3-5yr, 70% Hope)
- **Barriers:** "Still has time" mentality, hasn't tried everything yet, may still believe "wait until ready" advice
- **Triggers:** Pre-K deadline, peer comparison, pediatrician pressure
- **What works:** Education-first, mechanism explanation, Pre-K urgency hooks OK
- **What kills conversion:** Hard sell, "wait until ready" language, dismissing their hope

### Level 2 (Mixed, 5-7yr, 40% Hope)
- **Barriers:** Hyper-skepticism from 3-5+ failed methods, BCBA context, "I've tried everything" trauma response, financial sensitivity
- **Triggers:** OT/BCBA recommendation, Kelly Mahler/interoception research, specific science mechanism
- **What works:** Science-first, mechanism specificity, "Try-and-judge" framing, BCBA-respectful positioning
- **What kills conversion:** Hard sell, generic parenting advice, easy/quick language, BCBA criticism

### Level 3 (Non-verbal, 6-10+, 20% Hope)
- **Barriers:** Deep resignation, "he's too old/severe", Medicaid reality (price sensitivity), years of failed attempts
- **Triggers:** Upcoming life events (school, puberty), pull-up size change to adult diapers, sibling comparison
- **What works:** Dignity-first, late-trainer stories (Maureen Bennie 9.5), "Late is not never", very respectful, longer timeline promises (8 weeks)
- **What kills conversion:** Urgency manipulation, energy pushes, easy language, price pressure, any "fix" framing

### General Fallback (~50% Volume)
- **Barriers:** Unknown level identity, diagnostic-pending, label rejection, may not identify with any specific group
- **Triggers:** Symptom-first recognition, migration anchors
- **What works:** The "Whether...Or...If..." language pattern, universal symptom hooks, migration strategy
- **What kills conversion:** Level labels in copy, assuming specific child characteristics

## Key Conversion Insights Summary

1. **Phase 2 is the highest conversion opportunity** — "BIGGEST conversion lever in the entire avatar journey" (DOC1 L286). These moms have felt defeat and are searching for autism-specific solutions.

2. **Level 2 is the largest AND hardest segment** (40% of audience, 40% hope) — requires science-first, mechanism-specificity approach.

3. **The Trust Pyramid order is non-negotiable** — mechanism first, guarantee last. Reversing this kills conversion.

4. **60-day guarantee is the universal risk removal** — appears in Lever 3, Objection D3 counter, Trust Pyramid #5, and all conversion moments.

5. **Urgency works ONLY for Level 1** (Pre-K deadline). For Level 2-3, urgency is poison — especially Level 3 where dignity-first is non-negotiable.

6. **The Overwhelmed Mom converts on mess-reduction**, not speed. The Social Pressure Mom converts on social proof + mirror hooks. The Deadline Parent converts on time-bound outcomes.

7. **The 3+3 Bundle ($79.99) is the best conversion offer** — highest conversion rate across all segments.
