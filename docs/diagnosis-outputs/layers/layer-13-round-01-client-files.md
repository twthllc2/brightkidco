# Layer 13 — Trust Pyramid Extraction from Client Files (DOC1-DOC6)

## Overview

The Trust Pyramid is the hierarchy of trust elements parents need before they buy BrightKidCo products. The hierarchy is non-negotiable and follows this order from most to least important:

1. Mechanism Specificity
2. Peer-to-Peer Voice
3. Prior Failure Acknowledgment
4. Realistic Timelines
5. Guarantee / Risk Removal

This document extracts every trust-building element, credibility marker, and authority signal from all 6 DOC files, organized by pyramid tier (top to bottom).
