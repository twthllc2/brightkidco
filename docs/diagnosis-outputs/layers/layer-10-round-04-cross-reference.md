## 9. Summary of Findings

### Key Insights

1. **L1 (Mechanism) is the universal entry lever** — it's the primary lever in 3 of 4 phases and the #1 trust builder. Every email must explain WHY, not just WHAT.

2. **L4 (Lena) is the universal trust lever** — it's CRITICAL in every phase and every level. Lena's voice is the single point of trust across the entire ecosystem.

3. **L3 (Guarantee) is the universal closer** — it's Very High or Critical in every level. The 60-day window is the final conversion trigger.

4. **Phase 4 × Level 3 has the most gaps** — 5 of 10 identified gaps affect this combination. It's also the highest-LTV, most loyal segment. Investing in dedicated Phase 4/Level 3 levers would yield disproportionate returns.

5. **B4 (GI Issues) is massively underserved** — 376 Kommentare occurrences, the #1 objection, with no dedicated lever. A "GI Reality" lever could unlock significant Phase 3-4 conversions.

6. **The General Fallback (50% of volume) has no dedicated lever** — all 5 levers work at Medium. A universal "body signal" lever that doesn't require level-specific language could serve this segment.

7. **Voice rules create 7 conflicts with lever delivery** — the most critical being V9 (pull-ups-as-tool) vs L2 (pull-ups-as-villain) and V5 (forbidden "guaranteed") vs V8 (60-day guarantee). Each has a documented resolution.

8. **The objection × lever matrix shows L1 covers 4 of 11 primary objections** — making it the most versatile lever. L3 covers 2. L4 covers 2. L5 covers 1. L2 covers 1. This confirms L1's dominance.
