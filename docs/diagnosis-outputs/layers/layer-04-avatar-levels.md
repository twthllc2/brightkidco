Sarah is 33, lives in Denver, has two kids. Jake is 4, diagnosed Level 1 autism. He is verbal, speaks in sentences, knows letters, can count to 20. But potty training is not working reliably. Sarah tried Oh Crap two months ago. The first two days went well, then accidents piled up. Jake cried, she cried. She stopped after five days. Pre-K enrollment is in four months. If he is not trained, he cannot start. She now googles "autism potty training Pre-K." She knows there is a reason normal methods do not work. She is searching for autism-specific solutions.
