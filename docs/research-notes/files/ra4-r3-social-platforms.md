## Top Content Themes Analysis

### Highest Engagement Themes (rankled by cross-platform performance)

1. **"Day in the Life" / Raw Parenting Moments** — Unfiltered looks at autism family life. Emotional, relatable. Highest save/share rate.
2. **Meltdown/Sensory Overload Tips** — Practical strategies. High comment engagement ("this helped us so much").
3. **Milestone Celebrations** — First words, potty training wins, school achievements. Viral share potential.
4. **Autism Myths Debunked** — Educational content that challenges stereotypes. High comment engagement.
5. **IEP/School Advocacy** — Practical tips for navigating special education. Strong save rate.
6. **Sensory-Friendly Product Reviews** — Clothing, tools, toys. Natural product integration.
7. **Potty Training Autism-Specific Tips** — High search volume, underserved content area.
8. **Sibling Relationships** — Siblings of autistic children sharing their perspective.
9. **Autism in Girls/Women** — Growing awareness. Underrepresented content with high demand.
10. **Nutrition/Picky Eating** — Practical food strategies. High parent engagement.

### Content Format Winners by Platform

| Platform | Best Format | Avg. Length | Key Hook |
|----------|------------|-------------|----------|
| Instagram | Reels (tips) | 15-30 sec | "3 things I wish I knew..." |
| Instagram | Carousels | 5-10 slides | Infographic/educational |
| TikTok | Storytelling | 30-60 sec | Emotional moment + lesson |
| TikTok | Comedy | 15-30 sec | Relatable autism parent humor |
| YouTube | Vlogs | 10-15 min | "A day in our life" |
| YouTube | Tutorials | 8-12 min | "How to potty train an autistic child" |
