What "mini-wins" look like right now

At three weeks, progress can be subtle. You might not have a dry day yet. That's normal. Here's what counts as a win at this stage:

✓ He pauses mid-accident and looks down

✓ She pulls at her pants right before or after

✓ He says "wet" or "uh-oh" after the fact

✓ She tolerates the underwear for longer stretches

These might not feel like progress. They are. Each one is a signal that the interoceptive connection is beginning to form — that the brain is starting to register what the body feels.
