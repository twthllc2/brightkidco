While You Wait

A child who does not feel when they need to go is not being difficult — their nervous system is not delivering the signal in time. That is not a training problem. It is a wiring difference. And wiring can be built.

Two things to remember before Day 1:

  • Do not expect the toilet. Expect curiosity.
