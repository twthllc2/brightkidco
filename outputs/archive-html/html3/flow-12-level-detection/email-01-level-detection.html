The missing piece — the same for every child

Whether your child is almost trained but inconsistent, in the middle of sensory exploration with your OT, or has been in pull-ups for years without awareness change — the nervous system piece is the same. The "I need to go" signal is a body-brain connection. For autistic children, that signal can be delayed or muted. It's not that your child won't learn. It's that their brain hasn't been receiving the message in time.
