Here are a few things I've seen make a real difference at the 30-day mark:

  • Look for micro-wins. A pause, a look down, a hand-to-the-groin — these are pre-signals that the interoception is building. Celebrate those.
  • Keep the underwear as the anchor. Every time they wear it, the feedback loop fires. Day and night consistency matters more than perfect timing.
  • Drop the timer. If Oh Crap taught you to prompt every 15 minutes, that might actually be masking the emergence of their natural signal. Give space for them to feel it themselves.
  • Accept the plateaus. Progress isn't linear. A few quiet days don't mean it's not working. The Body-Signal Learning Layer™ is training a neurological pathway — that takes repetition, not perfection.
