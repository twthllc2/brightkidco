The question we hear most

"My child doesn't have a formal diagnosis.
Is this for us?"

Here's the short answer: You don't need a diagnosis.

You don't need a label. You don't need a doctor's note or a specialist's referral. If your child doesn't feel the body signal — or feels it too late — the Body-Signal Learning Layer™ can help.

Some children have a formal ASD diagnosis. Others have sensory processing differences that haven't been formally assessed. Others are still "figuring it out" — and that's okay.

The mechanism doesn't change based on a label. If your child's brain processes body signals differently — for whatever reason — the principle is the same: the signal needs to arrive before learning can happen.
