THE SCIENCE

The signal-timing gap

Interoception - the ability to feel internal signals like hunger or the urge to pee - develops differently in many autistic children. The 'I need to go' message travels from bladder to brain, and for some children, that pathway processes more slowly.

Pre-K is approaching. You still have time to build that pathway.
