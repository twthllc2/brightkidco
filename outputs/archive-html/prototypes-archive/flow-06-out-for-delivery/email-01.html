📋 Your Day 1 Prep Tip

When the box arrives, here's what we recommend:

  1. Open it together. Let your child see what's inside.
  2. Let them touch the fabric first. The tag, the seams — all of it. Sensory familiarity helps reduce resistance.
  3. Don't aim for the toilet today. Just wearing the underwear is the goal. Curiosity over compliance.

The Body-Signal Learning Layer™ starts working the moment they feel the difference between wet and dry. That awareness is the first step — and it doesn't require a potty in sight.

Most parents tell us the first sign of awareness comes within 7-14 days. For some children it's faster, for others slower. Both are completely normal. Progress is internal before it's visible.
