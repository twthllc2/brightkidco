THE SENSORY ENVIRONMENT: "THE BATHROOM IS THE PROBLEM"

For 90%+ of autistic children, the bathroom is a sensory war zone. Bright lights. Cold seats. Loud flushing. Echoes. Hand dryers.

Some adjustments that help:
• Dim the lights or use a night light
• Put a folded towel on the seat for warmth
• Let your child flush from outside the room
• Turn off the fan or overhead noise
• Use a portable potty in a preferred room initially
