GI ISSUES & WITHHOLDING
The withholding cycle — when it's medical, not behavioral

Chronic constipation. Pain. Withholding. Impaction. Hospital visits. The cycle is real, and it's the #1 medical complication in the autism community — autistic children are 4x more likely to have GI symptoms than neurotypical peers (McElhanon et al., 2014).

This is medical, not behavioral. No sticker chart, reward system, or behavioral protocol can address what's fundamentally a physical and neurological issue.

The Body-Signal Learning Layer works as an adjunctive tool — it doesn't replace your medical team's GI protocols. What it can do is help the body start recognizing the signal that gets lost in the withholding-anxiety cycle. Many parents report that once their child begins feeling the body's signals more clearly, the GI team has more data to work with.

We never promise a cure for withholding. But we do offer a tool that supports what your medical team is already doing — helping the nervous system recognize when it needs to go.

SENSORY REFUSAL
"He kicks me when I try to change him"

When a child has significant sensory defensiveness, putting anything new on their body can trigger a full meltdown. This isn't defiance — it's a nervous system responding to what it perceives as a threat.

Research shows that 90%+ of autistic children have atypical sensory processing (Leekam et al., 2007), and their brains don't habituate to uncomfortable clothing the way neurotypical brains do (Green et al., 2015, JAMA Psychiatry). Clothing discomfort builds over time rather than fading.

Our approach is different. We never expect you to just "put them on."

• Start by leaving the underwear next to the potty or in their room for several days
• Let your child examine them, touch them, hold them
• Try wearing them over a pull-up or specialty diaper for a few minutes at a time
• The BSL signal can still work worn over a diaper — the wetness penetrates
• Choose a preferred time — TV, tablet, calm activity
• Increase duration gradually: 5 minutes becomes 10, 10 becomes 20

Our underwear is 100% cotton, hypoallergenic, with a thin fit. The character designs (Melon Pop, Lily Pop, Fynn Fox, Coco Cloud) help make it feel like a normal choice rather than a "product."
