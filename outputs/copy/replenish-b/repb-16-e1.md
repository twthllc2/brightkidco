**If you've seen nothing yet:** That's not a sign it's not working. It's a sign the wiring is still in progress. Some kids need 8, 12, even 16 weeks before the first observable signal.

**If you've seen mini-wins:** The pathway is building. Keep going. The next 75 days often look very different from the first 75.

# PART 2 — FULL CREATIVE STRATEGY

## Big Idea

**"75 days in — the signal is wiring, even when you can't see it."**

The creative concept centers on the invisible progress of neural pathway-building. After 75 days of consistent Body-Signal Learning Layer exposure, the nervous system has been receiving information it was missing before — whether the observable results are dramatic or subtle. The email's emotional arc moves from "where are you at 75 days?" → "all of those are valid" → "the rotation is the engine" → "keep going at your own pace."

**Visual Metaphor:** A winding garden path that starts faint and becomes clearer — representing the interoception pathway being built over time. The path is never straight, never the same for any two families, but always moving forward.

**Core Emotional Promise:** Your 75 days of consistency are not wasted, regardless of observable results. The signal is wiring. Trust the process on your timeline.
