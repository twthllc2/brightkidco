## 3.3 Critical Objection Handling Rules

**Rule 1 — Never dismiss an objection.** Name it, validate it, reframe it.

**Rule 2 — The guarantee is the ONLY appropriate response to Objection D3.** "What if I get my hopes up again?" cannot be answered with more promises. The guarantee is the answer because it says: "You don't have to hope. You just have to try."

**Rule 3 — For L3, NEVER use objections as sales pressure.** "Your child deserves better" = guilt trip. "Time is precious — start now" = urgency. Forbidden. (Source: MASTER-DIAGNOSIS-COMPENDIUM.md §3.3)

**Rule 4 — For L2, objections require the full 6-element formula.** Missing any one element (real symptom recognition, mechanism spec, BCBA ack, realistic timelines, 60-day guarantee, permission-not-to-buy) breaks conversion. (Source: MASTER-DIAGNOSIS-COMPENDIUM.md §6.1)

**Rule 5 — GI/Withholding (B4) requires medical deference.** "Your medical team leads. We add alongside." Never position product as medical intervention. (Source: 30-customer-support-playbook.md §O3)

## 7.3 CTA Language Patterns — Permission Framework

**The core CTA philosophy across ALL levels:** Permission-not-to-buy. This is NOT a sales gimmick — it's the authentic trust-building mechanism that Giuliano demands. When a parent feels they have permission NOT to buy, they feel safe enough to buy.

**Permission CTA patterns (approved):**
- "60 days to try it. By your judgment." (Universal — strongest)
- "When you're ready." (Low aggression)
- "No pressure. Whenever works for your family." (L3)
- "See if it works for your child." (L1 medium)
- "If this fits, explore it. If not, no hard feelings." (GF)
- "You decide what's right for your child." (L3)
- "Add alongside for 60 days. No questions." (L3)

**Forbidden CTA patterns:**
- "Buy Now" (too aggressive, V4 violation)
- "Shop Today" (marketing voice)
- "Don't Miss Out" (fake scarcity)
- "Last Chance" (urgency manipulation)
- "Get Your Bundle" (too transactional for early emails)
- "Claim Your Discount" (marketing language)

(Source: GIULIANO-DEMANDS.md §7.1, V1-V24; 08-email-copy-brief.md §7)
