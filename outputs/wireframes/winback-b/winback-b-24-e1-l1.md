## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "The email is structured like a calendar page that's been flipped forward, a clock counting down the days until Pre-K, with the 3+3 Bundle positioned as the bridge between where she is and where she needs to be." A thin emerald timeline runs from the top of the email (Day 60) to the bottom (Pre-K start date), with the CTA button glowing at the final milestone.

**Contraption (Design Mechanism):** "The Pre-K Countdown Meter" — A custom vertical timeline illustration on the right side of the email. Three nodes connected by a thin line:
- Node 1 (top): "Today", small circle, cream #FBF7EB fill
- Node 2 (middle): "[X] Months Left", emerald #039902 filled segment shows time remaining
- Node 3 (bottom): "Pre-K Starts", destination marker with door icon

The emerald segment compresses or expands based on the actual months remaining. The tighter the gap, the more visual urgency. Illustration style: Editorial Luxury, clean vector lines, no fill except the active path. 1.5px stroke on all lines. Node circles at 8px diameter. No 3D, no gradients, no shadows.

**Emotional Arc Position:** This is the first re-engagement after 60 days. L1's primary lever is the Pre-K deadline — she signed up because of a concrete timeline. The email re-anchors on that original motivation without shaming the delay. The emotional arc: Recognition ("It's been two months") → Validation ("You haven't failed by waiting") → Offer ("The 3+3 Bundle is still here") → Risk Removal ("60 days to try it") → Permission ("If today isn't the day").

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-5 (Mirror Moment): Reflects L1's current state — "Remember your Pre-K deadline?" with clock icon.
- LETTER-02 (Handwritten Opener): Hook + validation with Pre-K deadline framing.
- ED3 (Interoception Explainer): Brief mechanism — not heavy science, L1 doesn't need it.
- SP5 (Counter Testimonial): Single L1 parent story — "bought the 3+3 bundle, week three he paused."
- ProductShowcaseMedium: 3+3 Bundle with Pre-K countdown meter.
- CTA-11 (Permission CTA): "Explore the 3+3 Bundle" — medium-low aggression.
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): Risk reversal, standard placement.
- DESDEEP-16 (Paper Texture): Subtle decorative warmth.

**Differentiation Note:** L1 gets the most visually prominent CTA because urgency is an accepted lever. The Pre-K Countdown Meter is an L1-exclusive visual element. Brighter accent colors than L2/L3 — emerald at full saturation, yellow as active warmth. Tighter spacing matches L1's higher engagement and faster decision speed (2-7 days).
