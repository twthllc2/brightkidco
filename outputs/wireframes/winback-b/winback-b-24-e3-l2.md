## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "The last tap on the door, from someone who means it." This email is the final contact in the Winback Path B sequence, the last time Lena reaches out to a never-bought L2 subscriber before letting them go. The creative strategy must honor the weight of that finality while leaving the door genuinely open.

For L2 (Lisa), the big idea is: *You have the right to walk away. But before you do, know that the science hasn't changed, the mechanism hasn't changed, and the 60-day guarantee hasn't changed. When you're ready, this is here.*

**Contraption (Design Mechanism):** "The Honest-Signal Closure Module" — A thin horizontal bar at the top of the email, colored Brand-Green #039902, tapering from full-width at the left edge to a single 12px round dot at the right edge. A visual metaphor for the contact closing while the option remains alive. Below the dot, micro-copy: "Still here when you are." in Soft Gray #9AA8A7, 11px. The E3 version of this signal bar shows the final stage of a 3-email taper (E1: full green bar → E2: fading → E3: tapered to dot).

**Emotional Arc Position:** E3 is the final email — "This my last email." For L2, the emotional arc is: Permission + truth → BCBA acknowledgment (one last time) → Mechanism summary → Realistic timelines → Permission-not-to-buy as the primary message.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-20 (Handwritten Letter Opener): Personal, peer-to-peer tone. Lisa reads this as a colleague, not a customer.
- LETTER-02 (Handwritten Opener): Long-form paragraph stack for L2's deliberate reading pace.
- SP — Testimonial: L2 parent testimonial ("After 4 years of prompt-dependent sits...") — the one substantial testimonial in this email.
- EDU-1 (Definition Block): Mechanism summary — Kelly Mahler, Nicholson 2019, SPARK 49% stat. Three citations.
- ProductShowcaseSoft: 3+3 Bundle with secondary 1 Pair link.
- CTA-4 (Gentle Invite): "Try the 3+3 Bundle, 60-Day Guarantee" — pill-shaped button.
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): Guarantee as trust anchor.

**Differentiation Note:** L2 uses the longest email (~780 words), the most science citations (3 in one mechanism block), the BCBA acknowledgment section (unique to L2), and the "Two-Dot" signature Easter egg (asymmetric dots representing Lena's two sons). The Signal Bar contraption is unique to E3 L2.
