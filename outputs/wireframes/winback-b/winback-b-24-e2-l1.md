**Big Idea:** "The signal doesn't lie." Sensory honesty over salesmanship. The new science citation (49-study review) acts as the structural spine — if the mechanism is real, the product is just the delivery system for that mechanism.

**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-17 Social Proof Hero leverages L1's analytical trust in numbers, LETTER-03 Letter + Pull-Quote for mechanism narrative, SP-13 Review of the Week for fresh testimonial, EDU-9 Tip Cards for mechanism breakdown, CTA-5 Product CTA for medium-aggression action, TRUST-1 60-Day Seal for guarantee, DESDEEP-6 The Color Block for visual warmth.
