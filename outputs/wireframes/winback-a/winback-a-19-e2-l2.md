## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** BCBA endorsement story — professional validation meets parent experience. The visual metaphor is the trust ladder: each section builds credibility through clinical authority (BCBA quotes) paired with relatable parent stories (Sarah's method-cycling journey).

**Emotional Arc Position:** Position 2 of 3 in Winback A. E1 was the gentle check-in. E2 is the pivot — BCBA endorsement re-engagement with data-driven re-engagement. This email answers "is there professional backing for trying again?" through the BCBA framing. E3 will be the final check-in.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-16 (Mechanism Tease): Opens with BCBA-context headline — "If it didn't click, here's what one BCBA tells her families." Mechanism tease per strategy map.
- LETTER-01 (Paragraph Stack): Long-form body copy for the ~1,050 word copy density. Paragraph stack supports BCBA quotes, data sections, and Sarah's story.
- TRUSTDEEP-5 (Trust Ladder): BCBA endorsement as primary social proof — building credibility through professional validation.
- EDU-8 (Mechanism Diagram): Neural pathway diagram — "ABA builds behavior, sensory adds awareness." Science-heavy per L2 calibration.
- ProductShowcaseFull: Full product presentation with 5+5 Bundle (L2 recommendation).
- CTA-4 (Gentle Invite): "When you're ready, 60 days to decide" — low aggression, BCBA-respectful.
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): Risk reversal.
- DESDEEP-16 (Paper Texture): Decorative paper texture for clinical warmth.

**Differentiation Note:** L2 is the most clinical variant — hope/realism 40/60. BCBA endorsement throughout. Method-cycling recognition (5 potty methods, 3 years ABA). Data-driven (82% stat, N=672). Semi-bold typography. Neutral color warmth. Balanced sections. "When you're ready" CTA.
