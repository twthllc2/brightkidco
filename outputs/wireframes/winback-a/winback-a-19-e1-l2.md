## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "The second time is different. Not because the product changed, because the nervous system needs enough reps." The core message: 90 days ago you tried the Body-Signal approach. If it didn't work, it's not proof the mechanism is wrong, it means the nervous system didn't get enough repetitions to start building the signal pathway. The variable that matters most for L2 children is consistency of wear time.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Position 1 of 3 in Winback A flow — The mechanism-first re-engagement. E1 is the first re-engagement after 90 days of silence. For L2 Lisa, the primary emotional driver is evidence and mechanism understanding. She respects her BCBA relationship and needs science-backed reasons to reconsider. The email respects her skepticism, honors her BCBA relationship, and offers data without pressure.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-16 (Mechanism Tease): Signals the science-forward approach. No lifestyle image — the mechanism is the hero. L2 Lisa responds to evidence, not energy. The mechanism tease hero opens with the "why it didn't click" question.
- LETTER-01 (Paragraph Stack): Clinical warmth, measured pace. L2 needs more text than L1. The paragraph stack allows for mechanism explanation, BCBA acknowledgment, and realistic timelines in a flowing format.
- TRUSTDEEP-5 (Trust Ladder): Trust built through evidence progression — mechanism → data → BCBA co-sign → guarantee. The trust ladder is the right structure for L2's evidence-driven decision-making.
- EDU-8 (Mechanism Diagram): Visual mechanism explanation — the Repetition Wave comparison. Intermittent vs. consistent wear shown as sine waves. L2 Lisa sees the difference immediately.
- ProductShowcaseFull: Full product showcase. The 3+3 Bundle is the mechanism partner. L2 needs to understand WHY the bundle matters (consistency = repetition = learning).
- CTA-4 (Gentle Invite): "Revisit the Body-Signal approach" — not "Buy Again" or "Re-order." The gentle invite respects L2's skepticism and permission-not-to-buy framing.
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): Compact trust anchor. The guarantee is the final risk removal.
- DESDEEP-16 (The Paper Texture): Decorative element — paper texture background. Signals clinical calm, the opposite of retail energy. Reinforces L2's evidence-first orientation.

**Differentiation Note:** L2 uses HERO-16 Mechanism Tease (no image, science-forward) vs. L1's HERO-10 Bold Declaration (lifestyle image, Pre-K urgency) and GF's HERO-9 Whisper Tone (no image, compassionate). L2's long-form testimonial (10-15 sentences) includes BCBA context. L2's color palette is muted sage/earth tones (not bright green/yellow). L2's mechanism section includes the Repetition Wave SVG comparison — unique to L2.
