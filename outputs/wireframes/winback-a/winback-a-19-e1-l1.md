## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "Starting again isn't starting over." After 90 days away, Sarah needs to hear that the investment she made wasn't wasted, that the time away was part of the process, not a reset. The big idea reframes a "failed attempt" as a "learning pause" and positions the restart as a continuation, not a do-over.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Position 1 of 3 in Winback A flow — The Pre-K urgency check-in. E1 is the first re-engagement after 90 days of silence. For L1 Sarah, the primary emotional driver is Pre-K deadline pressure. The email acknowledges the gap, validates the pause, and reframes it as part of the learning process — then channels her action orientation toward a specific restart plan.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-10 (Bold Declaration): Stronger than GF's Whisper Tone. L1 Sarah is deadline-driven and benefits from a more prominent, action-oriented hero. The "Pre-K is coming" headline directly addresses her primary emotional driver.
- LETTER-02 (Handwritten Opener): Personal letter format with peer opening. Lena as peer, not brand. The handwritten opener signals warmth without manipulation.
- SP-02 (Expert Endorsement): Two short L1 testimonials from relatable moms. Quick-read format for L1's faster scan velocity. Both testimonials are Pre-K-contextualized.
- ED1 (3-Layer Product Diagram): Visual mechanism explanation — the cross-section callout. L1 doesn't need deep science; a simple "here's why it works" visual suffices.
- ProductShowcaseFull: Full product showcase — the 3+3 Bundle is the hero product. Larger image, more detail. L1 Sarah wants speed and clarity.
- CTA-5 (Product CTA): Clear, action-oriented CTA. "Restart with the 3+3 Bundle" — direct, no ambiguity. Larger button (18px text) for higher tap visibility.
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): Compact trust anchor. The original guarantee still stands. Risk removal without sales energy.
- DESDEEP-8 (The Icon Story): Decorative element — icon-based visual storytelling. Reinforces the "pause is progress" theme with simple icons.

**Differentiation Note:** L1 uses HERO-10 Bold Declaration with Pre-K urgency framing, whereas GF uses HERO-9 Whisper Tone and L2 uses HERO-16 Mechanism Tease. L1's testimonials are short (3-8 sentences) vs. L2's long-form (10-15 sentences). L1's CTA is a full product button (CTA-5) vs. GF's permission CTA (CTA-11) and L3's exit close (CTA-12). L1 uses brighter accent colors (Yellow #F5C84B for urgency cues) and tighter section spacing (24px vs. 32px default).
