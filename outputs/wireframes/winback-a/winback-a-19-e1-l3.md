## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "We're not here to ask why you stopped. We're here to remind you what's here." The L3 winback email is the lowest-pressure re-engagement in the entire system. Maria has been burned by marketing promises for 7+ years. Her visual system is calibrated to detect manipulation. The email validates her choice to step back, gently re-explains the mechanism, and leaves the door open with zero pressure.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Position 1 of 3 in Winback A flow — The dignity-first check-in. E1 is the first re-engagement after 90 days of silence. For L3 Maria, the primary emotional driver is dignity preservation and exhaustion from broken promises. The email never asks her to try again. It reminds her what's here and lets her decide.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-9 (Whisper Tone): The lowest-intensity hero. No lifestyle image — just quiet text that signals "this isn't marketing." Appropriate for L3's 20/80 hope/realism ratio and ZERO urgency.
- LETTER-01 (Paragraph Stack): Spacious, measured pace. L3 needs room to breathe. Left-aligned paragraphs with generous line-height (1.7). No pull-quotes, no visual noise.
- SP-08 (Stacked Mini-Reviews): Not used in the primary layout. L3 doesn't need social proof — she needs validation of her own experience. The testimonial section is replaced with a simple validation statement.
- EDU-3 (Interoception Explainer): Gentle mechanism re-explanation. The shortest mechanism section of all levels. Just enough to remind her what the product does, not enough to feel like education.
- ProductShowcaseSoft: The softest product presentation. Product shown only after the mechanism. One pair only ($34.99) — no bundle upsell, no price comparison. The product is a tool, not a purchase.
- CTA-12 (Exit Close): The lowest-visual-pressure CTA in the entire system. No button. Text only with a single underline in brand green. "You decide. No timeline. No push." One CTA, one placement, once.
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): Compact trust anchor. The guarantee is mentioned in passing, not as a sales feature.
- DESDEEP-4 (The Minimalist Statement): Decorative element — minimal, non-distracting. Reinforces the quiet, dignified tone.

**Differentiation Note:** L3 is the only level with NO hero image, NO CTA button (underline-only text), NO bundle upsell, NO testimonials in the primary layout, and NO urgency indicators. The L3 wireframe uses the most whitespace, the most spacious typography (line-height 1.7), and the lowest visual pressure of all four levels. The CTA is a single text link — not a button, not a pair of buttons.
