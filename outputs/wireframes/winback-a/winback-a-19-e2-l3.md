## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** Late-trainer story as social proof — Maureen Bennie's son was 9.5 when he finally trained. The visual metaphor is the quiet dawn: soft, mature, spacious. No urgency, no pressure, just dignity and science delivered in a layout that breathes.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Position 2 of 3 in Winback A. E1 was the gentle check-in ("no guilt, no pressure"). E2 answers "has this ever worked for a child like mine?" through the Maureen Bennie late-trainer story and a peer L3 testimonial. E3 will be the final thought. This is the pivot from validation to quiet possibility.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-9 (Whisper Tone): Dignity-first headline — "Maureen Bennie's son was 9.5." Whisper tone per strategy map. No urgency, no pressure.
- LETTER-01 (Paragraph Stack): Long-form body copy for the ~600 word copy density. Paragraph stack supports the narrative flow — validation → late-trainer story → mechanism → offer.
- SP-07 (Counter-Testimonial): Maureen Bennie's story as the primary social proof — late-trainer credibility, not marketing. Counter-testimonial style per strategy map.
- EDU-1 (Definition Block): Mechanism explanation — "30-60 seconds of gentle sensation that lasts." Definition block for dignity-first framing.
- ProductShowcaseSoft: Soft product presentation with 1 Pair only. No bundle upsell, no math reframe per L3 compliance.
- CTA-2 (Soft Link): "See how the Body-Signal approach works" — mechanism-first CTA, zero urgency per L3 calibration.
- TRUST-4 (Dashed Policy Quote): 60-day guarantee as dashed policy quote — formal, dignified.
- 20.1 (Wave Divider): Organic wave dividers for visual breathing room.

**Differentiation Note:** L3 is the most dignified variant — hope/realism 20/80. Zero urgency throughout. Late-trainer story (Maureen Bennie, 9.5 years). 1 Pair only ($34.99), no bundle upsell. Bold typography. Cooler color warmth. Thorough sections. "Whether you try again or not" permission framing. No exclamation marks in body copy.
