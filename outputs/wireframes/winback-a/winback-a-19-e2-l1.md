## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** Pre-K success story as social proof — one mom's journey from "terrified he'd be the kid in pull-ups" to "his teacher said he's ready." The visual metaphor is urgency-with-hope: bright, playful energy channeled through a deadline-driven narrative.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Position 2 of 3 in Winback A. E1 was the gentle check-in. E2 is the pivot — testimonial-driven re-engagement with Pre-K urgency framing. This email answers "can this actually work before the deadline?" through Sarah's story. E3 will be the final nudge.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-10 (Bold Declaration): Pre-K urgency headline — "Pre-K is coming — here's the gap you can close." Bold, direct, deadline-driven per L1 calibration.
- LETTER-02 (Handwritten Opener): Personal, warm letter format. Lena's voice leading into Sarah's story. Handwritten opener matches L1's intimate, relatable tone.
- SP-02 (Expert Endorsement): Sarah's testimonial as the primary social proof — Pre-K success story, 82% awareness stat. Expert endorsement style per strategy map.
- EDU-3 (Interoception Explainer): Mechanism explanation — "It's not stubbornness. It's neurology." Science-light per L1 calibration.
- ProductShowcaseFull: Full product presentation with 3+3 Bundle. Pre-K deadline hook in CTA.
- CTA-5 (Product CTA): "See how other Pre-K parents made it work" — Pre-K urgency CTA, medium aggression per L1 calibration.
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): Risk reversal — 60-day guarantee.
- DESDEEP-8 (Icon Story): Decorative icon story for Pre-K narrative flow.

**Differentiation Note:** L1 is the most optimistic variant — hope/realism 70/30. Pre-K deadline framing throughout. Single parent focus (Sarah) vs GF's three-family pattern. Shorter, scannable sections (2-3 sentences). Brighter emerald accents (#00CC66). Larger CTAs. Warm, hopeful energy.
