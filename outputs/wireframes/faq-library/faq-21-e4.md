## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** A single letter from Lena that addresses the three objections every parent has voiced but no one has tied together — partner conflict, cost anxiety, and brand distrust. The email is structured like a conversation, three direct answers followed by a unifying insight, mirroring how Lena actually handles support emails.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Position 4 of 8 — Trust Rebuilding. The reader has already received three FAQ emails addressing mechanism, fear, and frustration. E4 pivots to the external blockers: the partner who says no, the budget that says wait, and the brand they've never heard of. The emotional arc moves from validation ("I hear all three") through risk removal ("60 days, your judgment") to permission ("This is your decision").

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-3 (Permission Open): Opens with Lena's voice naming the three questions — permission framing, not sales framing.
- LETTER-03 (Letter + Pull-Quote): Three objection sections in letter format. Each gets its own visual block with pull-quote emphasis on the key reframe.
- SP-04 (Trust Pills Row): 16,511 reviews · 4.9★ · 100,000+ parents — lightweight trust signals after the brand objection section.
- EDU-10 (Mechanism Box): Body-Signal Learning Layer mechanism callout, 30–60 second sustained sensation, positioned after the brand objection to answer "why should I trust you?"
- ProductShowcaseFull: 3+3 Bundle presentation with pricing ($79.99, ~$13/pair), placed after the cost objection to answer the budget question with facts.
- CTA-4 (Gentle Invite): Permission-framed CTA — "Shop the 3+3 Bundle" — zero urgency, zero countdown.
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): 60-day guarantee as risk removal across all three objections.
- 20.1 (Wave Divider): Signal-wave dividers separate the three objection sections visually.

**Differentiation Note:** This is the ONLY FAQ email structured around parental/external objections (partner, budget, brand) rather than child-symptom objections. The three sections mirror the three most common support ticket categories. L3 sensitivity: Cost section uses single-pair framing option alongside bundle — NO pull-up cost comparison (forbidden for L3 per S2 §3.1 C3).
