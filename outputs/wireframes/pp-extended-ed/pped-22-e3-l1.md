## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** Three micro-signs that mean the signal is getting through. The Pause, the Hand-to-Waist, and the Late Announcement are not failures — they are the interoception pathway forming. Pre-K is still months away. You're on schedule.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Doubt → Recognition → Relief → Quiet confidence. E3 is the "is this working?" email at the Week 5-6 plateau. It validates the doubt, names three specific signs, reinforces the mechanism, and closes with Pre-K anchor + permission.

**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-19 (Timeline Shock) leads with "Pre-K readiness is building" for L1-specific temporal anchoring. LETTER-01 (Paragraph Stack) delivers the three signs and validation copy. SP-06 (Before/After Testimonial) presents the L1 parent quote. EDU-10 (How It Works) reinforces mechanism. CTA-6 (Social CTA) is behavioral — "Trust the pause."

**Differentiation Note:** L1-specific: Pre-K urgency thread throughout. No BCBA protocol language (L1 parents don't have BCBA relationships). No L3 territory (no "adult diapers", no "years of failed"). Observation hook in subject line.

## 10. Rendering Notes

- **Email client considerations:** Sticker cards use table-based layout for Outlook compatibility. CTA button uses VML fallback for Outlook rendering. Gradient elements fall back to solid colors in unsupported clients.
- **Mobile responsive notes:** Single-column layout. CTA button goes full-width on screens < 600px. Hero image scales to 100% width. Section padding reduces to 16px on mobile. Three sign cards stack vertically with sticker treatment preserved. Minimum 48px tap targets on all interactive elements.
- **Accessibility notes:** All images have descriptive alt text. Color contrast ratios: body text #1F2D2F on #FBF7F1 passes WCAG AA (~12:1). CTA ink on green passes WCAG AA (~7.8:1). Footer muted #8A9B9D on ink passes WCAG AA (~4.6:1).
- **Special instructions:** PP-Extended Education E3 L1 is a nurture touchpoint — NO product pricing urgency. "Trust the pause" CTA is behavioral. Three sticker cards (Pause, Hand-to-Waist, Late Announcement) create visual rhythm and scannability. Pre-K anchor section is L1-exclusive — not present in GF/L2/L3 versions.
