## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "The Quiet Signals" — progress in body-signal learning doesn't arrive as a breakthrough. It arrives as micro-moments, a pause, a glance, a hand to the waistband, that most parents interpret as "nothing" but are actually the first bridge forming between body and brain.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Doubt → Recognition → Understanding → Permission → Continuation. E3 is the "are we there yet?" email at the Week 5-8 inflection point. It validates both seeing-something and seeing-nothing, provides 5 concrete micro-signals to watch for, and closes with the 60-day permission frame.

**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-19 (Timeline Shock) leads with the "five to eight weeks" milestone for temporal anchoring. LETTER-01 (Paragraph Stack) delivers the micro-signals list and validation copy. SP-06 (Before/After Testimonial) presents the single-parent quote pattern for GF. EDU-10 (How It Works) reinforces the mechanism explanation via the Invisible Bridge metaphor. CTA-6 (Social CTA) invites reply — educational, not transactional.

**Differentiation Note:** GF email uses symptom-based, inclusive copy. No level labels, no diagnosis language, no age specificity. "Nervous system processes signals differently" is the framing. Whether...Or...If... pattern in closing. 50/50 hope-realism balanced throughout.

## 8. Level Markers — Email-Specific Differences

Since this is the GF (General Family / Inclusive) version, the following design choices ensure cross-sub-group compliance:

| Marker | Implementation |
|--------|---------------|
| **GF — No Level Labels** | Zero level labels. No "Level 1/2/3", no "ASD", no "autism", no "diagnosis." Uses "nervous system processes signals differently." |
| **GF — Symptom Language** | "A pause mid-play. A glance at their pants. A hand reaching toward their waistband." — universal symptoms, no diagnosis framing. |
| **GF — Whether...Or...Pattern** | "Whether you're seeing small signs or waiting for the first one — you've done the hardest part." |
| **GF — Age Inclusivity** | No age references. "Your child" — inclusive of 3 to 10+. |
| **GF — 50/50 Hope-Realism** | "You might be wondering: Is anything actually happening?" (doubt) → "The answer is probably yes" (hope) → "Not every child follows that curve" (realism). |
| **GF — Low Urgency** | No deadline. No Pre-K clock. "If you need to pause and come back, that works too." |
| **GF — Medium Science Density** | Mechanism language without clinical jargon. "Body-Signal Learning Layer creates a sustained, gentle sensation" — accessible to all sub-groups. |
| **GF — Medium-Low CTA** | "Continue the journey" — soft, forward-moving without pressure. "60 days. By your judgment." |
| **GF — Sub-Group Coverage** | Diagnostic Entrants ✅, Diagnosis-Rejecters ✅, SPD/ADHD ✅, Overwhelmed Non-Selectors ✅, Cart/Browse ✅ |
