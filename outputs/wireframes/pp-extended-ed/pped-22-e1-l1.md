## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** The email is designed as a neural pathway visualization — a dotted signal line travels from the body through pulsing nodes, arriving at a glowing destination behind the CTA. The reader's eye follows the same path the interoception signal should travel: body to brain, confusion to understanding. Pre-K readiness is the anchor — the signal gap is what matters most right now.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Position 1 of the extended education sequence (D22+). Pre-K readiness reframe → Validate the "almost trained" struggle → Interoception pathway explanation → BSL as "turning the lights on" → Cautious hope for Pre-K timeline.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-17 (Social Proof Hero): Opens with peer-parent credibility, appropriate for L1's verbal, hope-intact parent who trusts other parents' experiences.
- LETTER-03 (Letter + Pull-Quote): Long-form letter with pull-quote emphasis on the key reframe moment.
- SP-13 (Review of the Week): Single parent testimonial showing Pre-K-relevant progress.
- EDU-8 (Mechanism Diagram): Visual interoception pathway with the "wiring, not training" explanation.
- ProductShowcaseMedium: Medium product presentation reinforcing the mechanism-to-product bridge.
- CTA-5 (Product CTA): More direct product CTA — L1 has Pre-K urgency.
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): Risk elimination.
- DESDEEP-6 (The Color Block): Warmer color block decorative element to differentiate from welcome flow.

**Differentiation Note:** L1 is Pre-K urgency driven. The clock tick easter egg (12 thin gray tick marks, one emerald at 3 o'clock) encodes the deadline anxiety without saying "you're running out of time." Warmer color palette. Tighter spacing (24px between cards). Larger CTA button (260px). Hope-forward design — green signals expand. Testimonial shows Jake (4, verbal, first pause after 3 weeks).
