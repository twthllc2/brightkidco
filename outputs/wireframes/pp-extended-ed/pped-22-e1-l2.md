## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "Why every method failed, neurology, not effort." The email is structured as a clinical evidence brief — the reader is an intelligent evaluator who has tried everything and needs mechanism depth, not motivation. The visual metaphor is a brain↔bladder communication diagram with a visible "gap" indicator, desaturated warm gray and cream tones. Could sit in a clinical handout.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Position 1 of the extended education sequence (D22+). Names every failed method → Reframes as neurological, not behavioral → BCBA complement frame (behavioral foundation + sensory layer) → Realistic timelines → Permission-not-to-buy.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-2 (Data Shock): Opens with a science-anchored data point, appropriate for L2's evidence-driven parent.
- LETTER-09 (Double Column): Structured double-column layout for L2's deep reading pattern — separates mechanism from validation.
- SP-05 (Before/After Testimonial): Single parent testimonial showing method-cycling parent's experience.
- EDU-11 (Science Citation): Primary citation block with Nicholson 2019 as the landmark reference.
- ProductShowcaseMedium: Medium product presentation — mechanism-first, product-as-resolution.
- CTA-11 (Permission CTA): Low-pressure, permission-framed CTA — L2 needs 7-21 days to decide.
- TRUST-2 (Guarantee Detail Cards): Detailed guarantee breakdown with "no questions, no hoops" framing.
- DESDEEP-5 (The Border Play): Subtle border-based decorative framing.

**Differentiation Note:** L2 is the largest audience (~40%). 40/60 hope/realism (realism-dominant). High science density — Nicholson 2019 cited by name, interoception mechanism explained in depth, prompt-dependency explained as neurological consequence. BCBA complement frame is critical — never criticizes BCBA work, positions BSL as additive layer. 8 specific failed methods named in the opening. Whitespace strategy: minimum 24px between sections, 40px before guarantee and permission sections.

## 8. Rendering Notes

### Level-Specific Calibration
- **Spacing:** 32px between sections, 40px before guarantee and permission — breathing room for thinking
- **CTA:** Muted green (70% opacity), no urgency framing
- **Accent brightness:** Desaturated — L2 responds to clinical calm
- **Urgency:** NONE — no deadlines, no countdowns, no "act now"
- **Hope/Realism:** 40/60 — realism-dominant, cracked hope
- **Layout density:** Low — more whitespace than L1 or GF
- **Font size:** Body at 16px desktop, 15px mobile (slightly smaller for deep reading)

### Compliance Notes
- **Reply prompt:** REQUIRED — P.S. offers BCBA fact sheet via reply
- **BCBA complement frame:** NEVER criticizes BCBA work — positions BSL as additive layer
- **Forbidden phrases:** ✅ No "quick/easy", ✅ No "fix/cure", ✅ No Pre-K urgency, ✅ No "don't give up"
- **L2 language:** Uses "ASD", "ABA" (L2-approved), clinical tone, evidence-based
