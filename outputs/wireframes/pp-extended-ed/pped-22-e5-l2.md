## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "The Plateau as Transfer Station" — A train that stops at a platform between stations, not derailed, not broken, not lost. The engine is still running under the floor. The transfer station is where the line switches tracks, where the local route becomes the express. L2 parents need to see the pause as construction, not collapse.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Final position in PP-Extended Education Flow (E5, D50). L2 parent has been wearing for 7-8 weeks. The plateau has hit. This email provides troubleshooting for the 3 most common plateaus, with mechanism reinforcement and BCBA-aligned strategies.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-2 (Data Shock) — leads with the neurological reframe, science-first approach
- LETTER-09 (Double Column) — double-column layout for the 3 plateau cards, scannable
- TRUSTDEEP-11 (The Research Bar) — science citations as social proof, L2-specific
- EDU-2 (Step Cards) — 3 numbered troubleshooting cards, each with symptom + strategy
- ProductShowcaseMedium — gentle product presence, not purchase-focused
- CTA-11 (Permission CTA) — "Ask Lena a question" (connection-based, not purchase)
- TRUST-2 (Guarantee Detail Cards) — full guarantee detail, L2 needs the reassurance
- DESDEEP-5 (The Border Play) — warm border decorative element

**Differentiation Note:** E5 L2 is the most science-dense and longest of the 4 variants (~800 words). The 3-plateau troubleshooting structure is the core deliverable. The unresolved equation Easter egg mirrors the L2 parent's feeling: all the ingredients are there, but the final step isn't written yet.
