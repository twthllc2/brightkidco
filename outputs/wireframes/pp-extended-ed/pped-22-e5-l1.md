## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "The pathways are forming, even when you can't see them." This email reframes Pre-K readiness from "we need to be perfect by September" to "we need to be aligned by September." Readiness is a TEAM effort, not a deadline for the child alone.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Final position in PP-Extended Education Flow (E5, D50). L1 parent has been wearing for 7 weeks. Pre-K is approaching. This email provides the school readiness protocol — teacher conversation template, pull-up-over-underwear system, morning/after-school rhythm. The arc: cautious hope → small wins → growing confidence → Pre-K readiness.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-17 (Social Proof Hero) — leads with social proof and Pre-K framing, not mechanism
- LETTER-03 (Letter + Pull-Quote) — letter format sustains Lena's personal voice, pull-quote anchors key validation
- SP-13 (Review of the Week) — single focused testimonial, Pre-K specific
- EDU-3 (Checklist) — numbered 3-step school readiness protocol, scannable
- ProductShowcaseMedium — 3+3 Bundle mention, gentle product presence
- CTA-5 (Product CTA) — product-focused but permission-framed, "Shop the 3+3 Bundle →"
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal) — lightweight trust anchor
- DESDEEP-6 (The Color Block) — warm color block decorative element

**Differentiation Note:** E5 L1 is the most action-oriented of the 4 level variants. The 3-step school readiness protocol (teacher conversation, pull-up system, morning/after-school rhythm) is the core deliverable. The P.S. offer (teacher info sheet) provides a concrete next step.

## 8. Rendering Notes

- **Email client considerations:** All backgrounds use solid colors (no CSS gradients in Outlook). Table-based layout for Outlook fallback. Mint badge border-radius 999px degrades to rectangle in Outlook — acceptable. Number badges use inline background-color for Outlook compatibility.
- **Mobile responsive notes:** Single-column layout at 375px viewport. Pathways illustration scales to full width. CTA button spans full-width. Section spacing compressed from 32px to 24px on mobile. Body text minimum 16px on mobile. Touch targets minimum 44x44px (L1 urgency makes frictionless click critical).
- **Accessibility notes:** All images require meaningful alt text. Color is never the sole carrier of information. Number badges use numeral + text, not color alone. CTA button text is sufficient without color context.
- **Dark mode:** Page bg → #1a1a1a, card bg → #2a2a2a, body text → #f5f5f5, CTA green → keep #039902. Pathways illustration: swap for dark-mode variant.
- **Special instructions:** The clock tick marks Easter egg must not be called out in copy — it's a subconscious Pre-K deadline signal. Step 2 (pull-up-over-underwear) is the most important section — it gets the Mint highlight. The teacher conversation template must be presented as a verbatim, copy-pasteable script. The P.S. teacher info sheet offer is the concrete next step that drives reply engagement.
