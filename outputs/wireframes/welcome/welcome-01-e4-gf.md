## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** Three different doors, one key — three distinct families standing before three different doors, but the key that unlocks all three is the same: body-signal learning. Universal mechanism.
**Emotional Arc Position:** Cautious hope → Realistic acceptance → Proof through testimonials → Incremental hope. E4 is the social proof email, proving the mechanism works across the full spectrum of children — from mild timing issues through method-cycling to long-term resignation.
**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-4 (Story Fragment) opens with a narrative fragment that draws the reader into the three-family story. LETTER-09 (Double Column) provides visual breathing room for the validation and mechanism sections with a two-column layout option. SP-09 (UGC Photo Grid) presents the three-family testimonials as a visual grid, reinforcing diversity. EDU-12 (Comparison Matrix) maps the 3-layer system against pull-ups for clarity. CTA-5 (Product CTA) with moderate urgency matches GF's permission-based tone.
**Differentiation Note:** GF email uses neutral warmth (#FAF8F5), no level labels (uses "Family 1/2/3" with symptom language only), Three-Family Pattern spanning full spectrum, and no urgency icons. The "Three Doors" visual micro-narrative easter egg appears only in GF. No calendar or clock imagery. Warm Sand background (#F5EDE3) in creative strategy replaces clinical white.
