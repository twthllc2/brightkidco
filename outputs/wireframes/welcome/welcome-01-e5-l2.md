## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** The Signal You've Been Waiting For — a suspension bridge connecting two landmasses across an impossible gap: failed methods on one side, first moment of body awareness on the other. The bridge is the body-signal underwear.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Skepticism → Acknowledgment → Reframe → Cautious Trust (midpoint pivot email in Welcome Flow)

**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-13 (Scientific Hook) establishes credibility immediately for hyper-skeptical L2; LETTER-09 (Double Column) handles the long-form mechanism explanation with scannable density; SP-02 (Expert Endorsement) reinforces the science with researcher citations; EDU-8 (Mechanism Diagram) visualizes the 3-layer system; TRUST-2 (Guarantee Detail Cards) provides concrete 60-day promise without pressure

**Differentiation Note:** The only email in the Welcome Flow that uses a suspension bridge SVG contraption as its visual anchor. Denser and more science-heavy than any other level variant. Zero urgency signals — the most permission-based CTA in the entire flow.
