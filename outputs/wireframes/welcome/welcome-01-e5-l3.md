## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** The Signal Bridge — this email exists in a quiet, patient space, not a sales funnel. Progress is not a destination but a single quiet moment: a body receiving information it has never received before. The Patient Witness archetype.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Skepticism → Acknowledgment → Reframe → Cautious Trust (midpoint pivot email, L3 dignity-first variant)

**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-9 (Whisper Tone) sets the subdued, non-marketing tone from the first line; LETTER-07 (Greeting Card) provides warm, spacious text layout for the narrative arc; SP-07 (Counter-Testimonial) uses raw parent quotes without marketing polish; EDU-1 (Definition Block) explains interoception as a simple definition, not a complex diagram; TRUST-4 (Dashed Policy Quote) provides the guarantee as a quiet policy statement, not a bold claim

**Differentiation Note:** The only L3 email that uses the "Between Two Hands" diptych contraption. Zero exclamation marks anywhere. The most muted CTA in the entire Welcome Flow — warm taupe, not forest teal. White space exceeds 40% of total vertical layout.
