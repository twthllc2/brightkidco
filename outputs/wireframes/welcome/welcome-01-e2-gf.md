## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "The signal finally being heard" — each section strips away the noise of failed methods, revealing the single missing element: interoception. A dotted neural pathway arcs from a question mark (confusion) to a glowing "aha" node behind the CTA, visualizing the journey from "I don't know why nothing works" to "Now I understand the mechanism."

**Emotional Arc Position:** E2 is the reframe pivot in the Welcome Flow. E1 validated the problem; E2 names the failed methods and explains why they couldn't work. Naming failed methods → Absolving guilt → Offering mechanism → Hope through understanding.

**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-1 (Curiosity Gap) opens with the universal hook "Why sticker charts fail some kids" — invites curiosity without assuming knowledge or diagnosis. LETTER-04 (Letter + Image Break) delivers the failed-methods recognition with a visual pause. SP-02 (Expert Endorsement) grounds the mechanism in research authority (Wiggins et al. 2022, Nicholson et al. 2019). EDU-4 (Myth vs Fact) makes the interoception gap concrete. ProductShowcaseMedium introduces the product as mechanism solution. CTA-3 (Curiosity Code) matches GF's moderate urgency — "See how body-signal learning works" with permission-framed secondary link. TRUST-2 (Guarantee Detail Cards) provides risk reversal. 20.1 (Wave Divider) creates organic breathing between sections.

**Differentiation Note:** GF uses inclusive, neutral warmth — no level-coding, no urgency micro-text, no countdown visuals. The palette is universally warm-educational, using teal as the universal "learning" color. Signal-path dots are muted teal (#039902 at 70% opacity) — noticeable but not urgent, the visual equivalent of GF's 50/50 hope/realism calibration. No L1's bright coral, no L2's diagrams, no L3's maximum whitespace.

## 8. Rendering Notes

- **Email client considerations:** Klaviyo platform — inline CSS only. Signal-path dots use CSS animation (3s animated dashed stroke) for Apple Mail; static fallback (dotted line, terminal node filled) for Gmail/Outlook.
