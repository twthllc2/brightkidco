## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "The Aha That Changes Everything" — the moment the parent realizes their child isn't stubborn, their nervous system is just processing differently. The interoception gap reframe is the emotional hinge of the Welcome Flow.

**Emotional Arc Position:** E2 is the validation-to-mechanism pivot. E1 validated the problem; E2 is where the mechanism "clicks." Naming failed methods → Absolving guilt → Offering mechanism → Hope through understanding.

**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-5 (Mirror Moment) mirrors the parent's own experience back at them. LETTER-01 (Paragraph Stack) delivers the failed-methods recognition in clean, scannable paragraphs. SP-01 (Yellow Quote Block) grounds the mechanism in real parent voices. EDU-4 (Myth vs Fact) makes the interoception gap concrete and falsifiable. ProductShowcaseMedium introduces the product inline after the aha moment. CTA-4 (Gentle Invite) matches L1's moderate urgency without aggression. TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal) provides risk reversal for the fast L1 decision window. UTIL-02 (Divider Bar) creates clean breathing between sections.

**Differentiation Note:** L1 uses +15% saturation on coral accents, tighter 24px section gaps, larger 48px CTA button, bold 32px hero headline, scrollable testimonial cards (3 visible), and the "Pre-K prep starts here" urgency micro-text above CTA. This is brighter and more energetic than L2/L3 variants.
