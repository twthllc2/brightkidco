## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** Your BCBA built the bridge. This lays the cable the signal travels on.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Second email in the Welcome flow — naming failed methods → absolving guilt → offering mechanism → hope through understanding. The reader has received E1 (hook/welcome) and is now ready for the deeper reframe from "behavioral problem" to "neurological gap."

**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-13 (Scientific Hook) opens with research-backed authority matching L2's skeptical, research-driven mindset. LETTER-03 (Letter + Pull-Quote) delivers the long-form narrative L2 needs for their 7-21 day decision cycle. SP-02 (Expert Endorsement) reinforces BCBA/OT credibility. EDU-10 (How It Works) delivers the 3-layer mechanism in a structured format L2 can share with their therapy team. ProductShowcaseMedium introduces the product inline without a hard sell. CTA-5 (Product CTA) provides a clear but low-pressure conversion path. TRUST-2 (Guarantee Detail Cards) breaks down the 60-day guarantee into discrete trust signals. 20.2 (Dot Pattern Divider) references the 3-layer system motif.

**Differentiation Note:** More science-dense than L1 (which uses simpler illustrations). More structured and diagrammatic than L3 (which uses organic wave dividers and no product in E2). The 3-layer cross-section diagram is specific to L2 — L1 gets a simpler ring stack, L3 gets a top-down texture view.
