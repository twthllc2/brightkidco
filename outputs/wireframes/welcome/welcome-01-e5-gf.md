## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** The method failed you. The mechanism won't.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Fifth position in Welcome Flow — the moment of reckoning. After E1 (hook), E2 (discovery), E3 (decision), E4 (wisdom), E5 directly addresses accumulated skepticism. Parent arrives with all their disappointment; this email meets it honestly.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-7 (Counter-Intuitive Truth) — leads with the reversal that methods failed, not the parent. Sets up the reframe.
- LETTER-03 (Letter + Pull-Quote) — letter format sustains the peer-to-peer voice. Pull-quote creates scannable waypoints.
- SP-05 (Before/After Testimonial) — two side-by-side quote cards for desktop, stacked on mobile. Multiple perspectives bridge GF sub-groups.
- EDU-6 (FAQ Blocks) — the Three Objection Doors are inherently Q&A. FAQ blocks let each objection expand with reframe inline.
- ProductShowcaseFull — hero product moment after mechanism education has landed.
- CTA-5 (Product CTA) — full-width green button after full mechanism + social proof + guarantee has earned the ask.
- TRUST-2 (Guarantee Detail Cards) — standalone guarantee section before CTA for maximum risk-reversal.
- 20.3 (Gradient Band Variants) — section dividers between major blocks.

**Differentiation Note:** E5 is the only email in the Welcome Flow that directly confronts failure. E1-E4 build understanding; E5 is where the parent brings accumulated disappointment to the table and gets a direct answer. The Three Objection Doors contraption is unique to E5 — no other email uses this progressive-reveal objection pattern.
