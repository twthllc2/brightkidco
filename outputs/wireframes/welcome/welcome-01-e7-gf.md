## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** Training wheels for the nervous system — a sensory bridge that provides the missing feedback, consistently, until the brain learns to recognize the body signal on its own.

**Emotional Arc Position:** Email 7 of 8. Second-to-last education push before the final decision email (E8). Reader has received 6 prior emails spanning introduction, mechanism tease, decision framing, and objection handling. E7 is the deep mechanism explainer: the "how it actually works" email that converts curiosity into understanding.

**Component Selection Rationale:**
- HERO-16 (Mechanism Tease): Opens with curiosity about the mechanism, matching the subject line "How the body-signal learning actually works"
- LETTER-03 (Letter + Pull-Quote): Long-form educational copy with embedded pull-quotes for scanning parents
- SP-05 (Before/After Testimonial): Cross-level testimonials from L1, L2, L3 parents showing the spectrum of micro-wins
- EDU-9 (Tip Cards): The BSL 3-Layer System presented as stacked cards, each layer a distinct visual unit
- ProductShowcaseFull: Hero product shot with the 3+3 Bundle, pricing, feature pills, and dual CTA
- CTA-5 (Product CTA): Full-width dark CTA with lime-green text, primary action
- TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal): Understated shield icon with "60 days. Your judgment." — trust signal without sales pressure
- 20.3 (Gradient Band Variants): Warm amber-to-cream gradient transitions between sections

**Differentiation Note:** GF E7 is the most text-heavy email in the Welcome Flow. Unlike E1-E6 which use shorter formats, E7 presents the full mechanism explanation. The GF variant is distinguished from L1/L2/L3 by: (1) cross-level testimonials spanning all three levels, (2) symptom-only language with no level labels, (3) universal "training wheels" metaphor accessible to all education levels, (4) dual CTA (bundle + single pair) giving Phase 1 readers a low-commitment entry point.
