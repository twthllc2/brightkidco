## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** "The Signal That Finally Arrives" — a dotted neural arc travels from the left margin across the body, growing brighter as the science unfolds, arriving at a solid glowing emerald destination behind the CTA.
**Emotional Arc Position:** Validation → Stat normalization → Mechanism clarity → Cautious hope (mid-flow, E3 of 8)
**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-10 (Bold Declaration) opens with a strong, direct statement matching Sarah's Pre-K urgency; LETTER-01 (Paragraph Stack) delivers clean, scannable body copy; SP-01 (Yellow Quote Block) provides L1-specific social proof; EDU-2 (Step Cards) breaks the 3-layer system into digestible steps; ProductShowcaseFull reveals the product; CTA-5 (Product CTA) with emerald button; TRUST-1 (60-Day Seal) provides safety; UTIL-01 (Gradient Band) creates visual transitions.
**Differentiation Note:** L1 uses brighter emerald (#039902) at full saturation, tighter spacing (24px vs 32px), Pre-K deadline framing, single-level conversation between Lena and Sarah (no three-family comparison). Hidden clock easter egg signals urgency without countdown pressure.
