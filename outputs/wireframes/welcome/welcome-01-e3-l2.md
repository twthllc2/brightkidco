## 2. Strategy Box

**Big Idea:** The bridge across the interoception gap — the parent finally sees WHY nothing has worked and HOW this mechanism is different.
**Emotional Arc Position:** Validation → Stat normalization → Mechanism clarity → Cautious hope (E3 = credibility-building, the "proof" email)
**Component Selection Rationale:** HERO-16 (Mechanism Tease) opens with a scientific diagram tease that hooks the L2 research-driven reader. LETTER-09 (Double Column) allows the interoception science to breathe with a two-column layout on desktop. SP-02 (Expert Endorsement) leverages Kelly Mahler's authority for the BCBA-aware audience. EDU-10 (How It Works) breaks the 3-layer system into a clear mechanism diagram. ProductShowcaseFull presents the 3+3 Bundle at $79.99 as the hero product moment. CTA-5 (Product CTA) is action-oriented but low urgency. TRUST-2 (Guarantee Detail Cards) provides the risk reversal L2 needs most.
**Differentiation Note:** Unlike L1 (Pre-K urgency) or L3 (dignity-first), L2 uses a scientific-diagram-driven hero, dense stat blocks (19%, 49%), and a blueprint-style product layer breakdown. This email reads like a research briefing, not a sales pitch.

## 8. Rendering Notes

- **Email client considerations:** Apple Mail (iOS/macOS): Full CSS animation support for hero pulse and stat fade-in. Gmail app: No style block support — all enhancements degrade gracefully to static. Outlook desktop: No animation support, static fallback on everything. Samsung Mail, Yahoo: Partial support, test before deployment.
- **Mobile responsive notes:** Hero section collapses from 50/50 two-column to single-column stacked below 480px. Textile visual becomes full-width 200px band. All body text minimum 16px on mobile. H-padding reduces from 30px to 20px. ProductShowcaseFull sub-cards stack vertically. CTA button maintains 44px minimum tap target.
- **Accessibility notes:** Navy text (#1A2A3A) on off-white (#FAF8F5): contrast ratio 11.2:1, exceeds WCAG AA. Dark grey body (#4A4A4A) on off-white: 8.1:1, exceeds WCAG AA. White text (#FFFFFF) on amber button (#D4893D): 4.8:1, meets WCAG AA. White text on navy footer (#1A2A3A): 7.5:1, exceeds WCAG AA. All images require descriptive alt text. CTA button has 44px minimum tap target.
- **Special instructions:** No urgency indicators — no countdown timers, no "limited time" flags, no red/orange alert tones. L2 decision cycle is 7-21 days; pressure backfires. The guarantee section is intentionally larger and more prominent than L1/L2 baseline — L2 needs the risk reversal visual more than any other segment. Science-visual density is higher: 2-3 diagrams, stat blocks, or data callouts per scroll-length. The "40+ Methods" Easter egg is implemented as a hyperlink with dotted amber underline that opens a tooltip footnote (fallback: footnote at email bottom in Source Citations section).
