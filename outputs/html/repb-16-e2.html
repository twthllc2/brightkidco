### MECHANISM, Why 90 Days Is a Milestone (Mechanism 50%)

Ninety days of Body-Signal Learning Layer exposure means the nervous system that picks up signals slowly has had sustained, gentle feedback, 30 to 60 seconds of sensation per wear, to start building a connection that never existed before.

The science is clear: body signal differences in autism are real and measurable (Barmpagiannis & Baldimtsi 2025, 49-study systematic review). The internal "I need to go" signal doesn't arrive the way traditional methods assume. Pull-ups wick away the sensation entirely, the brain gets nothing to work with. The Body-Signal Learning Layer provides the feedback that pull-ups remove.

Some brains need 2 weeks to register the signal. Others need 12 weeks or more. Both are within the normal range for autistic children. (R5, Timeline Flexibility)
