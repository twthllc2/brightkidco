Three roadblocks — what to do about each
I hear from a lot of parents around this point. The underwear arrived. Then something got in the way.
 
 
Roadblock 1: Your child won't wear them
Many autistic children experience tactile defensiveness. Try 20-30 minute intervals with a preferred activity.
 
 
Roadblock 2: No awareness signs yet
Try increasing wear time to 3+ consecutive hours. The brain needs sustained repetition over hours, not minutes.
 
 
Roadblock 3: Regression after early progress
Not starting over. The nervous system is recalibrating. The pathways you built are still there. Hold steady.
