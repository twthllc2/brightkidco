When your package arrives

1. Wash before first wear. The cotton inner layer creates the signal. Wash once on gentle cycle, no fabric softener — it coats the cotton and dulls the sensation.

2. Let your child hold it. Open the package together. Let them touch the fabric, get familiar. Some kids need a day or two of sensory familiarity before wearing. That's normal.

3. Start with 1-2 hours at home. No prompts. No pressure. Just wear. The gentle signal after every accident is how the brain learns to connect sensation to action.
