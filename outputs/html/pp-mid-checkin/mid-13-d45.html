If progress is slower than you hoped
Whether seeing tiny signs or nothing at all, 45 days in, here's what we know about this stage.
 
 
The absence of visible progress is not the absence of progress
The interoception pathway builds through repetition. Every time your child wears the underwear, the nervous system receives new input.
Both my sons went through this. One showed awareness at 5 weeks. The other took almost 12. Both got there.
 
Three things to check
1. Wear consistency: 2-3 hours uninterrupted daily?
2. Bathroom stress: Reduce prompts. Let the underwear teach.
3. Regression: Not starting over. Pathways are still there.

The science reason
