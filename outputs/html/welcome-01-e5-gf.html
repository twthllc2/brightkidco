Objection 3, "Too old / too severe"

- GF catch-all: "Whether your child is 3 or 10, verbal or non-verbal, diagnosed or still wondering"

- Covers L1 (young, verbal), L2 (mixed), L3 (older, non-verbal), AND GF (undiagnosed/wondering)
