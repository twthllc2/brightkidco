HOW IT WORKS

The search for a solution often leads to methods that assume the signal is there. Forty-nine peer-reviewed studies confirm body signal differences are a well-established feature of autism. The ‘eighth sensory system’ works differently for him.

His brain isn’t ignoring the signal. The signal never arrived in a way his brain could recognize. Research shows autistic children show significantly diminished body awareness — this isn’t a training gap. It’s a neurological difference no sticker chart can solve.

Think of it like training wheels for the nervous system.
