## 1. THE LANGUAGE BANK PHILOSOPHY

### Why Verbatim Matters

Email copy that uses the parent's own words sounds like a friend talking. Marketing language sounds like an ad. The language bank is the difference between trust and skepticism.

### The 3 Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **NEVER fabricate quotes** — If you cannot find a real quote, flag it. Brand-trust-critical.
2. **NEVER rewrite into "nicer English"** — "I sound like I'm talking to my dog and I hate it" is MORE trustworthy than "Parents may find themselves engaging in repetitive verbal prompts."
3. **NEVER sanitize the rough edges** — Dialect, hesitations, emphasis, humor-as-coping: these are the trust anchor.
