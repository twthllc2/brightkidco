### 2.3 Key Competitive Pricing Insights

**Insight 1: BKC's single-pair pricing ($34.99) is premium.**
- Higher than UpAiry ($20-30), Thirsties ($18-25), Big Elephant ($28.89)
- Comparable to Peejamas ($35), lower than Snugkins system ($80+)
- Justification: Proprietary 3-Layer System™, ASD-specific trial (672 children), premium 100% cotton

**Insight 2: BKC's bundle pricing ($12-13/pair) is highly competitive.**
- Lower per-pair than every direct reusable competitor's single-pair price
- Comparable to mass-market Gerber ($3-5/pair in multi-packs) but with 3x absorbency and body-signal mechanism
- The 5+5 Bundle at $119.99 ($12/pair) is the best per-unit value in the direct reusable category

**Insight 3: No competitor offers a money-back guarantee for training underwear.**
- Pull-Ups/Pampers offer "satisfaction guarantee" but these are retailer-managed
- BKC's 30-Day Potty Trained Promise™ and 60-Day Calm Progress Guarantee™ are unique in the category
- Less than 1% of parents use the guarantee — low risk, high trust signal

**Insight 4: Bundles are underutilized across all competitors.**
- Only Snugkins offers system bundles (product + alarm)
- GroVia and Big Elephant offer multi-pack savings but no structured bundle tiers
- BKC's 3-tier bundle system (Single / Best Value / Max Value) mirrors proven decoy effect pricing

**Insight 5: Free shipping is the #1 expected offer.**
- Source: campaign-free-shipping skill — free shipping is #1 reason for cart abandonment
- UpAiry, Super Undies, Thirsties, GroVia all offer free shipping over threshold
- BKC offers free worldwide shipping on ALL orders — a significant advantage

### G.4 Cart Recovery Discount Testing Protocol

Test 1 (E2): No discount vs 10% off — measure recovery rate and AOV
Test 2 (E3): 10% off vs 15% off — find optimal depth without margin erosion
Test 3 (Upsell): Discount on upgrade vs discount on original — which drives higher total AOV

> **Benchmark:** Abandoned cart discount on E2/E3 (not E1) improves recovery by 15-25% (industry data from campaign-free-shipping skill). BKC should test within this range.
