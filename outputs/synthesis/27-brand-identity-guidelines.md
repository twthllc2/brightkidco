### 1.2 The Core Problem We Solve

The Autistic Parent avatar does NOT have a potty training problem. They have a **Body-Signal Translation Problem**. The child doesn't understand the chain: Body Signal → Action. Traditional training assumes the child CAN feel the signal. Autistic children often cannot.

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║              THE BODY-SIGNAL LEARNING LAYER™ — CORE MECHANISM                ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                              ║
║  ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐  ║
║  │  DISPOSABLES (Pull-Ups, Pampers, GoodNites):                          │  ║
║  │  Child pees → Diaper wicks away → Feels completely dry →              │  ║
║  │  → Brain learns NOTHING → No body awareness develops                  │  ║
║  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘  ║
║                                │                                              ║
║                                ▼                                              ║
║  ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐  ║
║  │  BRIGHTKIDCO 3-LAYER SYSTEM™:                                         │  ║
║  │  Layer 1 (Inner): Soft cotton, gentle "uh-oh" sensation               │  ║
║  │  Layer 2 (Core):  3x absorption — protection WITHOUT blocking         │  ║
║  │  Layer 3 (Outer): Leak-resistant — prevents mess                      │  ║
║  │                                                                        │  ║
║  │  Child pees → Feels gentle "uh-oh" → Brain connects "I peed =         │  ║
```
