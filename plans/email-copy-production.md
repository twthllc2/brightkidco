## Purpose

The strategy phase produced the blueprint (8 documents, 793KB). Now we need **two things per email — produced together, in one file, by one agent:**

1. **Full email copy** — subject line, preview text, body, CTA, Lena sign-off, footer
2. **Full creative strategy** — big idea, layout, contraption, color narrative, typography, motion, level-calibrated visual direction

These are **not separate phases**. The agent who writes the copy also defines the creative vision — because the emotion, tone, and level calibration are already determined while writing the words. Separating them creates gaps.

The S2 document (2-full-email-copy-strategy.md) contains the copy strategy (hooks, angles, subject lines). The trimmed design skills reference (`consolidated-design-skills-trimmed.md`) gives the agent the vocabulary to describe premium visual direction. Together they produce one integrated deliverable.
